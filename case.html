HECAN! is a horror web series created by me, Anais Ram, the plot follows: A YouTuber in 2008 finds a copy of a long lost Sonic the Hedgehog game, once he starts playing creepy things start happening around him that makes him question his sanity and who he can trust.

Wyatt Jameson is a 17 year old YouTuber who, under the username "SonicCentral2", makes videos on the topic of the famous videogame series Sonic the Hedgehog, he finds himself in a mystery as he receives coordinates to a nearby forest, as he explores said forest he finds a metal box deep underground, in it were various confidential documents, but also the long lost sequel to "Sonic & Knuckles".
